ABOUT THE PROJECT

At the time of the Great Second Advent Movement (1840-1844), a chart was created based on the book of Daniel and Revelation. The chart devised by Charles Fitch and Apollos Hale helped preachers explain Daniel's prophecy to their listeners. God also directed the leaders of the movement through Hab 2:2 to draw a large poster with the chart that was used in large meeting to preach prophecy. Adapting the message of Habakkuk to our days, God inspires us to create an interactive table on the network so that every person on the planet who has access to the Internet can interactively study the Apocalyptic prophecy of the book of Daniel and the book of Revelation through this table.
